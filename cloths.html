Traditional Dress of Bhutan

Complete set of Bhutan national dress items

Gho

Traditional knee-length robe worn by Bhutanese men.

Kira

Traditional long dress worn by Bhutanese women.

Rachu

Decorative scarf worn by women during ceremonies.

Kabney

Scarf worn by men showing rank and respect.

Kera (Belt)

Woven belt used to tie Gho and Kira securely.

Tsho Lham

Traditional boots worn with Bhutanese national dress.

Tego

Short jacket worn by men and woman under the Gho during formal events.

Koma

Decorative brooch used to pin the Kira at the shoulders.
